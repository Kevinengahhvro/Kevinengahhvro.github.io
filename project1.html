Year 10 Work Experience with USYD Aeronautical Engineering

I found out that there is journal that they were publishing papers that 13-18 year olds had made with proper peer reviews and so on. I had lost passion for the idea and brought myself back to the drawing board. I found inspiration in wind tunnels and how car manufacturers designed cars so then I got invested in aerodynamics. With this I cold emailed a USYD professor who specialised in aerodynamics, he offered to help me but encouraged me to learn CFDso that I could simulate better! He suggested a study on car spoilers and determining using experimental and theoretical values to show what car spoiler reduces lift and drag.
